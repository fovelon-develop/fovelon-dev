Common questions
Your assistant keeps answering, you keep coaching.

The most frequent questions visitors ask before they book a call or buy – answered automatically, logged as leads, and ready for your follow-up.

Will the AI invent answers?
No. When confidence is low, the assistant stops, asks for contact details and sends the question to your inbox instead of hallucinating.
How long does setup take?
Most users get a trained assistant live on their site in under 15 minutes. You paste the script, import your FAQ, and watch the first conversations roll in.
Can I review past conversations?
Yes. Every chat is stored in your inbox with tags, timestamps and source page – so you can review what people ask before you tweak your offer.
Does it support multiple websites?
You can run separate assistants for each domain or funnel, all feeding into the same inbox with clear tags for the source.
Can I use it as a simple contact form?
Absolutely. You can keep only lead capture on and route all messages to email while you experiment with AI replies in the background.
What if I update my pricing or FAQ?
Update your FAQ or key pages and the assistant will pick up the new information on the next sync – no redeploy needed.
Can I export my leads?
Yes. Export all conversations, tags and contact details to CSV so you can upload them into your CRM or newsletter tool.
Does it work with WordPress / Webflow / custom code?
If your site can load one JavaScript snippet, the widget works. No special plugins or frameworks are required.
Can I pause the assistant without removing the widget?
Yes. You can switch to “collect only” mode where visitors still see the widget, but all replies are manual from your side.
Will it slow down my website?
The script is lightweight and loads asynchronously, so it doesn’t block your main content or hurt your Core Web Vitals.
